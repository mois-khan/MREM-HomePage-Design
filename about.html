Academic Excellence Since 2008

Malla Reddy Engineering College & Management Sciences (MREM) was established in 2008 under the auspices of Vemula Raghava Reddy Lakshmamma Educational Society. Our campus is located in a verdant 10-acre serene environment on the Hyderabad – Nagpur National Highway (Medchal Road).

The college provides an ideal setting amidst natural surroundings, lush green and free of pollution, fostering an atmosphere of focused learning and innovation. We are affiliated with JNTUH, Hyderabad and approved by AICTE.
