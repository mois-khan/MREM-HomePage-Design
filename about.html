About MREM

Our Legacy

Established in 2008 under the Vemula Raghava Reddy Lakshmamma Educational Society, Malla Reddy Engineering College & Management Sciences (MREM) has grown into a premier destination for technical education in Telangana.

Affiliated with JNTUH and approved by AICTE, our institution is committed to providing a holistic learning environment. We blend rigorous academic programs with practical industry exposure, ensuring our students are not just graduates, but industry-ready professionals.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To emerge as a premier institution in technical education to produce competent engineers and management professionals contributing to industry and society through excellence in academics and research.

Our Mission

  • Impart updated pedagogical techniques with supportive learning environment.
  • Cultivate a culture of interdisciplinary approach and innovative ecosystem.
  • Instil ethical, social and environmental values through community engagement.

Infrastructure

Library

A state-of-the-art library with over 50,000 volumes, international journals, and digital resources to support research and academic excellence.

Transport

A fleet of modern buses connecting all major parts of Hyderabad and Secunderabad, ensuring safe and timely commute for students and staff.

Medical Centre

Dedicated health center with qualified medical professionals and emergency support available 24/7 on campus.
